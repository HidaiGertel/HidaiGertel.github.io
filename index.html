Idea

Why html for graphic designers?
  • html is a graphic design tool, made for creating website pages.
  • It can be used directly, by anyone.
  • The basics of HTML and CSS, can be learned and practiced in a short time, and are sufficient for making beautiful creative pages.
Join the course, and learn how to express your work online, using these tools.

Design

How does it work? A group of (up to) 8 students meet with a tutor once a week for 2h. What do we teach? In the course of six weeks you will learn:
  • To add any type of content to your page: Text. Galleries. Videos. Links. Maps and more!
  • To control every element's position and size.
  • To make responsive pages, competible with tablets and mobiles.
  • To combine typographies, colors, backgrounds, effects and animations to complete the feel of the page.
How much does it cost? The price of the course is 120€. By mentioning a friend's name in the comment you give him/her a 20€ discount. (mentioning each other is also allowed)
