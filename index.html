Why html for graphic designers?

◦ At its core, HTML is a graphic design tool,
made for creating website pages.

◦ It can be used directly, by anyone.
Especially graphic designers.
Without programming, templates or 3rd-party services.

◦ The basics of HTML and CSS,
can be learned and practiced in a short time,
and are sufficient for making beautiful creative pages.

Join the course,
and learn how to express your work online,
using these tools.

Concept


What will you learn?

The course consists of 6 meetings - 4 classes and 2 workshops.
You will learn how to:

◦ Add any type of content to your page using HTML.
Text. Galleries. Videos. Links. Maps and more...

◦ Control every element's position and size.

◦ Make responsive pages, competible with tablets and mobiles.

◦ Combine fonts, colors, backgrounds, effects and animations to complete the look and feel of the page.
